About Us

DJ Enterprises Established in 2005, Our head office is located in Vizag and branches in Mumbai and Kakinada to cater to each region of India. It has established standards for providing Reliable Products, providing Rational Pricing, and providing Responsive Services to a wide range of clientele. Digvijay Sahoo is the sole proprietor of DJ Enterprises which is mainly dealing with ferrous and non-ferrous scrap and minerals.

Trading

At Dj_Ent, we deal with a multitude of commodities from across the globe including; ferrous & non-ferrous scrap, ferroalloys, base and minor metals and minerals. With a well-defined global footprint, Dj_Ent has access and exposure to a plethora of markets, commodities and geographies. Our easy access to raw materials and intermediates used in various manufacturing processes makes us one of the most preferred suppliers globally.

Dj_Ent is driven by the philosophy of CREATING VALUES for its stakeholders at both the ends of the supply chain by partnering with them, with a continuous focus on building mutually beneficial long-term supplier and client relationships.

Our global experience, multicultural competencies and extensive technical expertise has propelled our rapid yet steady growth throughout the years.
